# Code of Conduct

All contributors, users, and participants in TinyLink-Public are expected to follow this Code of Conduct. Our goal is to maintain a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate to everyone.
- Communicate openly and constructively.
- Focus on the project, not on individuals.
- Report issues or concerns responsibly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments.
- Threats, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Spamming or disruptive behavior.
- Sharing inappropriate content.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness any unacceptable behavior, please report it to the repository maintainers by opening an **Issue** or contacting the project team directly. All reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take actions including warnings, temporary suspension, or banning individuals from the repository or related community spaces to enforce this Code of Conduct.

## Acknowledgements

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.

Thank you for helping us keep TinyLink-Public a safe and welcoming community!
